Study Background
The classic Pisay scholar keeps a watchful eye on their grades, even to the point of risking their own mental health. After three years of rigorous learning, has this affected their own worth and purpose in life? Let us find out as we analyze the data that we had collected from the four classes. Our goal here is to identify the relationship between the self-image of the scholar with respect to their actual and apparent performance in school. We will be looking at the self-rating of their academic performance, their self-worth, and the average GWA in the last two-quarters of Grade 8.
